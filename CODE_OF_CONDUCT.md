# Code of Conduct

Reikai is a small personal project. The expectations are simple:

- Be respectful in issues, pull requests, and discussions, and assume good faith.
- No harassment, hate speech, personal attacks, or sharing anyone's private information.
- Keep it on-topic and constructive.

The maintainer may edit, remove, or lock any comment, and may block accounts that don't follow this, at their discretion.

To report a problem, contact [@unseensnick](https://github.com/unseensnick). If it's sensitive, reach out privately rather than opening a public issue.
